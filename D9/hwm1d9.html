BENVENUTO SU TOMBOLA

    RULES
  • Every card has 15 random numbers
  • A random number will be selected from the bag
  • Each time the random number is found in your card, it will be marked
  • Mark two or more numbers on the SAME row in your card and you will win a prize
    PRIZES
  • AMBO: two numbers, same row.
  • TERNA: three numbers, same row.
  • QUATERNA: four numbers, same row.
  • CINQUINA: five numbers, same row.
  • TOMBOLA: all fifteen numbers in your card.
